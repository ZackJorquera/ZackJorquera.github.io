About Me

My name is Zackary Jorquera, although I go by Zack. I'm a Ph.D. student at UCSC in the Algorithms and Thoery group working under Alex Kolla. My primary interest is in theoretical computer science. However, I have experience in many other fields of computer science, such as computer vision, systems programming, and more.


I did my undergrad at the University of Colorado at Boulder, where I Graduated Summa Cum Laude majoring in Computer Science with a double Minor in Applied Math and Pure Math.
