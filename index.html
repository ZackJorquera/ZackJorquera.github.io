About Me

My name is Zack. I'm from Colorado, with half my family originally from Chile. I'm a Ph.D. student at UCSC in the Algorithms and Theory group working under Alex Kolla. I love math, computer science, and the intersection of the two; theoretical computer science. I also very much enjoy teaching and hope to teach many classes during my Ph.D.


I did my undergrad at the University of Colorado at Boulder, where I Graduated with a BS in Computer Science and a double Minor in Applied Math and Pure Math.
