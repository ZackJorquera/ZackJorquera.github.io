Reading Groups (That I Organized, at UCSC)

  • Fall 2023: Representation theory of the symmetric group with applications to TCS. We primarly followed Representation Theory of Finite Groups by Steinberg and The Symmetric Group by Sagan and we read these two papers: Symmetric Groups and Expander Graphs and Low Influence Functions over Slices of the Boolean Hypercube Depend on Few Coordinates.
  • Fall 2024: The PCP Theorem and Hardness of Approximation. We followed a combination of these lecture notes by Venkatesan Guruswami and Ryan O'Donnell, these lecture notes by Luca Trevisan, Irit Dinur's paper, and this paper by Jaikumar Radhakrishnan, which improves on some of the analysis in the graph powering step. In total, we proved the PCP theorem and looked at applications of the PCP theorem and the Unique Games Conjecture to hardness approximation.
